About us

Welcome to Square Eyes, the ultimate online destination for film enthusiasts and aspiring filmmakers. Square Eyes was founded by a group of friends who recognized the immense talent and creativity present in the world of independent filmmaking. Inspired by the desire to support and promote these talented individuals, they embarked on a mission to create a space where filmmakers could directly connect with film-lovers.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to connect film-lovers with the next generation of filmmakers. We believe that every filmmaker deserves a platform to showcase their work, and every film-lover deserves access to a diverse range of captivating and thought-provoking movies. Square Eyes provides an unique online store experience, where filmmakers can upload their original films for customers to discover, purchase, and download. We curate a wide selection of films across various genres, ensuring that there is something for every taste and preference. Whether you are a filmmaker or a film-lover, we invite you to join Square Eyes and be a part of our growing community. Together, let's celebrate the magic of cinema, support emerging talent, and forge meaningful connections through the films we love.

“I’m so greatful for this platform. It has truly opened my opportunities in the film-making world. I didnt know where to start and how to connect to an audience. Now i have thousands of watchers from all over the world” - Steve Olsson / Film producer and uploader at Square Eyes
